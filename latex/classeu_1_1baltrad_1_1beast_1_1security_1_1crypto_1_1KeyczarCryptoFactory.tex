\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}

It assumes a certain structure from your keystore\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item private keys must be stored in \$key\+Store\+Root/\$name.\+priv
\item public keys must be stored in \$key\+Store\+Root/\$name.\+pub
