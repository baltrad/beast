\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Used for grouping a number of reporters into a combined version. 

For example a system health check can be performed with system=bdb,db and there are two different reporters, one for bdb and the other for db. Then you can create a grouped status reporter that will check all reporters for their status. \begin{DoxyAuthor}{Author}
Anders Henja 
\end{DoxyAuthor}
